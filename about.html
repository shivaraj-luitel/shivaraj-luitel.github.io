About Me

I am Shiva Raj Luitel. I hold a Bachelor’s degree in Electronics and Communication Engineering and a Master’s degree in Information Systems Engineering. Since 2017, I have been actively involved in teaching undergraduate-level students, helping them build a strong foundation in engineering and technology. Alongside teaching, I currently work as a Computer Engineer at the Department of Foreign Employment, where I contribute to technical systems and digital solutions. I am passionate about technology, education, and continuous learning, and I enjoy applying my knowledge to real-world problems.
